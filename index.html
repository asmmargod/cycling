People love our cycling adventures

I had the best 3 days of my life. And an awesome trip in an ambulance

I loved every bit of the 3 days of cycling. Absolutely loved the idea of not having a guide hence I got lost and rode onto someone's garden and destroyed a bunch of roses. This resulted with being chased by a peasant with a pitchfork and a visit to an emergency room. What fun way to spend 3 days and the nurses in the hospital were so hot!

We were so pleased to see a proper road roller. We were happy with it being stationary

We cycled together as a group for many years but we never knew it could be so much fun! Our sight impaired guide was so much fun to ride with! He kept falling over and cycling into potholes and ditches. We laughed so hard that one of us was taken to hospital with a ruptured bladder. A rupture bladder! Who knew this could happen to human body.

We were so tired after the week that we slept for two weeks afterwards

It was a blast having cycling without sleep for whole week, the mute guide kept kicking us so we would not fall asleep, the blind guide would walk into us all the time and the deaf guide screamed at us with passion greater than we ever experienced. Sadly he apparently lost his voice in the end so we don't know if he'll be able to be part of this guide group.
